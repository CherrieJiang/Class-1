This is a portrait of Mao Zedong.

In Chinese, stone relief called "Hua Xiang Shi" which means the stone engraved with someone's portrait. But not only some famous one's portrait, mostly, ancient Chinese people in order to record some important events and moment by using this way. People also in that period use these easy pattens to represent some simple words. Then use these pattens to fomulate some pictures and express some massages until the appearence of real words. According this character, I try to establish a new rule to reform the black and write gray relation then use this new relation to establish new patten. As a result, use modern technique to rebuilt the stone relief.
